## 1. Executive Summary

**PDF Company Stamper** is a browser-based SaaS application designed to digitize the traditional workflow of physical rubber stamping and signing for Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs). Unlike competitors that require file uploads to remote servers, our solution processes documents entirely within the user's browser, ensuring 100% data privacy and compliance with data sovereignty requirements.

## 2. The Problem

In the modern digital administrative landscape, specifically in regions like Malaysia and Southeast Asia, the "Company Stamp" (Cop Syarikat) remains a mandatory verification step for invoices, receipts, and official letters.

Current solutions have significant friction points:
1.  **The Analog Loop:** Print -> Stamp -> Sign -> Scan -> Email. This wastes paper, ink, and time.
2.  **Privacy Risks:** Online PDF tools (e.g., SmallPDF, ILovePDF) often require uploading sensitive financial documents to foreign servers.
3.  **Complexity:** Professional tools like Adobe Acrobat Pro are expensive and have a steep learning curve for basic administrative staff.

## 3. The Solution

**PDF Company Stamper** offers a "Drop, Stamp, Download" workflow that mimics the physical action but in a digital environment.

*   **Zero-Upload Security:** Utilizes WebAssembly (WASM) and HTML5 Canvas to manipulate PDFs locally. No data leaves the device.
*   **Instant Gratification:** No registration required for basic use.
*   **AI-Enhanced:** Integration with Google Gemini/OpenAI to auto-format addresses and summarize content, reducing data entry errors.
*   **Batch Processing:** Stamp hundreds of invoices in seconds, saving hours of administrative work.

## 5. Monetization Strategy (Future Roadmap)

While the core utility is free to drive adoption (Product-Led Growth), revenue streams will be introduced in Phase 3:

1.  **Pro Tier ($5/month):**
    *   Cloud sync for stamp templates across devices.
    *   Team management (Admin controls stamp usage).
    *   Audit logs (Who stamped what and when).
2.  **Enterprise API:**
    *   API access for ERP systems to auto-stamp generated PDFs.
3.  **White Labeling:**
    *   Licensing the engine to corporate intranets.
